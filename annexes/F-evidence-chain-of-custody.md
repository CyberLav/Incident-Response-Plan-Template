|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

## Principles

- Volatile evidence collected before non-volatile (memory/network state before disk images)
- Every access to stored evidence after collection is logged, not just the initial collection
- Hash values recorded at collection and re-verified before any analysis, to prove
  integrity if evidence is later needed for legal proceedings
