# Annex E — Decision Log Template

Opened at [Identification, Step 4](../02-identification.md#24-step-4--formal-activation)
and kept live for the duration of the incident. Every decision that affects scope,
containment approach, business impact, or disclosure gets logged here. This is the record
that makes the response legally defensible after the fact.
